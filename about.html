Our story

Oscar and Ben met while studying at The University of Westminster in 2014. Having worked together for two years they teamed up to form 4D Island out of a desire to take hypothetical projects into the real world. They share similar design values, using data to create proposals that are informed and highly forward thinking while remaining humane.

Having worked for some of London’s top practices and working on a range of local and global projects of varying scale, Ben has a particular interest in the use of integrated technologies in the built environment and the use of social driven data inputs to inform better design.

Ben Pollock

Architect / Co-founder
BA, March, PGDip, ARB, RIBA

Oscar has spent much of his early career working on some of the most complex projects at Wilkinson Eyre Architects, his interest in complexity has expanded into urban systems with a particular focus on how to use data to improve outmoded ‘2D’ urban design.
